Ottawa covid-19 wastewater surveillance

The National Capital Region is one of the first communities in Canada to conduct daily wastewater readings to help inform its community response in the fight against covid-19, thanks to innovative research from the CHEO Research Institute (CHEO RI) and the University of Ottawa (uOttawa).

Learn more about wastewater epidemiology and its role in covid-19 surveillance on Ottawa Public Health’s website.

The science supporting wastewater surveillance is rapidly evolving. The plots are currently for research only and presented to the public for discussion.

*New cases (reported date) is the date reported by the laboratory. New cases (episode date) is the earliest onset, test or reported date.

*Viral copies data is normalized using a seasonally stable fecal biomarker; pepper mild mottle virus (PMMoV). See methods for more details.
